THINGS
I BROKE.

and what they taught me.

BUG_001

THE WEBSITE WORKED LOCALLY. DEPLOYMENT SAID NO.

LESSON

Environment variables, build paths, and production environments matter. "Works on my machine" is not a feature.

BUG_002

EVERY USER COULD SEE EVERYTHING IN THE DASHBOARD.

LESSON

Authentication is identifying who the user is. Authorization is deciding what they can see. Do not mix them up.

BUG_003

THE MOBILE DESIGN LOOKED PERFECT. ON MY LAPTOP.

LESSON

Responsive design must be designed from the start, not patched at the end with media queries.

BUG_004

I CHANGED ONE THING. FIVE THINGS BROKE.

LESSON

Spaghetti code is fast to write but impossible to maintain. Component structure and separation of concerns save hours of debugging.
